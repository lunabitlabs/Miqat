Miqat

Miqat is a simple and accurate prayer times app designed to help you stay connected to your prayers with clarity and focus.

Features

  • Accurate prayer times based on your location
  • Clean and distraction-free design
  • Offline support
  • Designed exclusively for iPhone

Privacy Policy

Miqat respects your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Location (optional): Miqat may request access to your device location only to calculate accurate prayer times. Location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted.

Miqat does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Miqat does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.

support@miqat.app
